# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Responsibilities

### Project Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate and fair corrective action
- Removing, editing, or rejecting comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that don't align with this Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently banning contributors for behaviors deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful

### All Community Members

All community members are responsible for:

- Reading and understanding this Code of Conduct
- Treating others with respect and kindness
- Reporting violations when observed
- Supporting a healthy community environment

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including:

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at:

**Email:** conduct@anjan.dev

All complaints will be:
- Reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly
- Kept confidential
- Responded to within 48 hours

### What to Include in a Report

When reporting, please include:

- Your contact information
- Names (real, nicknames, or pseudonyms) of involved parties
- Description of the incident
- Any supporting documentation (screenshots, links, etc.)
- Whether the incident is ongoing
- Any other relevant context

### Enforcement Process

1. **Receipt** - Acknowledgment within 24 hours
2. **Review** - Investigation and assessment (1-7 days)
3. **Decision** - Determination and action plan
4. **Notification** - Involved parties notified of outcome
5. **Appeal** - Option to appeal within 7 days

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction

**Community Impact:** Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome.

**Consequence:** A private, written warning providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning

**Community Impact:** A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**Consequence:** A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved for a specified period. This includes avoiding interactions in community spaces and external channels. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Community Impact:** A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence:** A temporary ban from any interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period. No public or private interaction with the people involved is allowed during this period. Violating these terms may lead to a permanent ban.

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Community Impact:** Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment, or aggression toward individuals or groups.

**Consequence:** A permanent ban from any public interaction within the community.

## Specific Guidelines

### Code Reviews

- Focus on code, not people
- Explain *why*, not just *what* to change
- Suggest alternatives with reasoning
- Appreciate good work
- Be patient with learning curves

### Issue Discussions

- Stay on topic
- Be constructive
- Assume good intentions
- Provide context and examples
- Search before posting duplicates

### Pull Requests

- Follow contribution guidelines
- Respond gracefully to feedback
- Keep discussions professional
- Close unneeded PRs promptly
